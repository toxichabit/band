ABOUT US

Born in the underground scene of 2025, Toxic Habit emerged from the ashes of conformity with one mission: to bring back the raw, unfiltered energy of pure punk rock.

We don't play by the rules. We don't ask for permission. We create music that hits you in the face and makes you feel alive. Three chords, maximum attitude, zero compromises.

Our sound is a molotov cocktail of fast drums, screaming guitars, and lyrics that matter. If you're looking for radio-friendly pop punk, keep scrolling. This is the real deal.
